Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Prestij Mobile ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Important: Prestij Mobile collects minimal data and only uses Google Analytics for basic app usage statistics. We do not collect, store, or share personal information beyond what is described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Authentication Information

Our App serves existing users who log in with pre-established credentials. We do not collect additional personal information during the login process beyond what is necessary for authentication purposes.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This service automatically collects certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
  • Technical data (app version, crash reports, performance metrics)
  • General location data (country/region level only)

Note: Google Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual users from this data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide authentication and core app features to existing users
  • App Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve the user experience
  • Technical Support: To identify and fix technical issues and bugs
  • Performance Monitoring: To monitor app performance and stability

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • Google Analytics: As described above, anonymized usage data is processed by Google Analytics in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users will be notified of any changes)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

Authentication Data: Retained only as long as necessary to provide app functionality
Analytics Data: Automatically processed and retained by Google Analytics according to their data retention policies

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about data we may have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking through your device settings
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate data

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. Google Analytics data is processed according to Google's global data processing practices and privacy commitments.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
